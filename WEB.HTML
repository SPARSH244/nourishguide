Healthy Food = Healthy Life

🍎 What is Nutrition?

Healthy Food

Nutrition is the process of taking in food and using it for growth, energy, repair, and maintaining good health.

Fresh Fruits

Fruits provide vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants that keep our body strong and prevent diseases.

Vegetables

Vegetables improve digestion, boost immunity, and supply important nutrients for a healthy body.

💊 Essential Vitamins

Vitamin A

Good for eyesight and skin.
Sources: Carrots, Mangoes, Spinach.

Vitamin C

Boosts immunity and helps wound healing.
Sources: Oranges, Lemon, Guava.

Vitamin D

Strengthens bones and teeth.
Sources: Sunlight, Milk, Eggs.

🥗 Balanced Diet

What is a Balanced Diet?

A balanced diet contains carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, fiber, and water in the right amounts.

Healthy Plate

🥦 Vegetables 50%
🍚 Whole Grains 25%
🍗 Protein 25%
🥛 Dairy & Water

Healthy Habits

✔ Drink 8 glasses of water
✔ Exercise daily
✔ Eat fruits & vegetables
✔ Avoid junk food
